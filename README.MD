This project was the beginning to enable pay by bank instead of card in apps through Open Banking. It would have cut out middleman fees, allow open money tracking and eliminate fraud bigtime.

## 2 Things

### Open Banking is a lie
### TransferWise let me down

## Explain
#### Open banking is a lie
I started this project after being rejected by Open Banking. TrueLayer provides open banking but closed fees so it's not open and it feels like a group of people picking who to work with. - You are not the boss of me!

#### Transferwise let me down
So I went with Transferwise because they have APIs I can use... but it doesn't work. They have APIs that don't work and they expect me to chase them like they're my saviour. I do technology not politics! 

#### Says it all
This just goes to show the world is for Open Banking. It is being held back by people who don't want to fully release it and think they own technology. It's sad but people should use technologies like Bitcoin that are not controlled by people,politics and gives freedom to creators.
